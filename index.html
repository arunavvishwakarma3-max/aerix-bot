What is AERIX?

AERIX is a premium all-in-one Discord bot built for security, automation, and engagement. From real-time anti-nuke protection that stops raiders in their tracks, to a full music streaming system, custom tickets, moderation, leveling, economy, giveaways, and tournaments — AERIX replaces 5+ bots with one.

Powered by discord.js v14 and SQLite, it delivers blazing-fast performance with zero external dependencies. Trusted by servers of all sizes.

Bot to Rule Them All

Everything You Need

A full toolkit for server management, entertainment, and community engagement.

🛡️

Anti-Nuke

Rate-limits bans, kicks, channel & role deletions in a rolling 60s window. Auto-punishes raiders with configurable actions. Whitelist trusted users & roles.

🎵

Music System

High-quality YouTube streaming via yt-dlp. Full queue management, loop modes (off/song/queue), shuffle, volume control, and an interactive now-playing panel.

🎫

Ticket System

Per-user ticket channels with category organization. Claim and close functionality. Configurable support roles and max tickets per user.

🔨

Moderation

Complete toolkit: ban, kick, mute, warn, purge messages, slowmode, and channel lock/unlock. Full permission-based access control.

📈

Leveling & XP

Earn XP through chat messages and voice activity. Configurable level-up rewards, role assignments, and leaderboards. Up to level 1000.

🪙

Economy

Full economy with daily/weekly/monthly rewards, work, rob, gambling, slots, lottery, and bank system with interest rates.

🎉

Giveaways

Create giveaways with prizes, duration, and optional required roles. Auto-draws winners and announces results. Enter and leave with a click.

🏆

Tournaments

Team registration, bracket generation, and match tracking. Perfect for competitive gaming communities and events.

⚙️

Auto-Moderation

Configurable limits on caps, mentions, emojis, duplicate messages, lines, and newlines. Keeps your chat clean automatically.

👋

Welcome / Goodbye

Customizable join and leave messages with optional canvas-generated welcome images. Make new members feel at home.

🔐

Reaction Roles

Button-based role assignment system. Users can self-assign and remove roles with a single click. Full role menu support.

📊

Logging & Info

Detailed audit logging for message edits/deletes, joins/leaves, bans, voice state changes, and more. Server info, user info, role info commands.
